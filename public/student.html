Earthquake Safety

1. DROP, COVER, and HOLD ON.
Immediately drop to your hands and knees. Cover your head and neck with your arms. If a sturdy table is nearby, crawl underneath it.

2. Stay away from glass.
Avoid windows, hanging objects, mirrors, and tall furniture that could fall.

3. Evacuate when safe.
Wait until the shaking stops before moving to the designated open area.

Fire Protocol

1. Stay low.
Smoke rises. Crawl on the floor where the air is cleaner.

2. Check doors for heat.
Use the back of your hand to feel a door before opening it. If it's hot, do not open it.

3. Use the nearest exit.
Proceed calmly to the nearest fire exit. Do not use elevators.

Flood / Water Safety

1. Move to higher ground.
Head to the second floor or the designated evacuation center (Mendoza Court).

2. Avoid electrical hazards.
Do not touch electrical equipment if you are wet or standing in water.

3. Do not walk through floodwater.
It may be deeper or faster-flowing than it appears.
